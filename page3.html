PROJECT OUTLINE:

Navigating TV's Influence: A Student's
Guide to Academic Progress


This will be a documentary that will delve into the intricate connection between television viewing and students' academic performance, exploring the reasons for engaging in TV watching and examining its potential consequences for their educational achievements. Understanding this relationship can assist our target viewers, which are students, facilitators, and parents, in encouraging a balanced approach to television consumption and academic endeavors, thereby minimizing adverse effects.

This documentary will be recorded beforehand and then streamed live on Streamlabs Obs because it will be more fitting and appropriate for us to use as it's beginner-friendly. This will last for about 20–40 minutes so that the participants or viewers won’t get bored and will pay full attention to the information that will be discussed in the documentary. The speakers of this documentary will be the students in Group 3 of ONL Passion 10 2 from APEC Schools, having at least 2 members as its minimum. We will also be gathering opinions from the same section about our topic; this can be through the survey that we conducted last quarter or by inviting students to be our guests and be a part of our documentary. We would also like to invite or search for some reputable speakers to support its credibility. The sourcing for this speaker can be through connections or videos online. This soon-to-be stream will then be exposed to various social media platforms to promote it so that we can reach our target audience and number of participants. 


The broadcasting software we selected, "Streamlabs Obs," is user-friendly for beginners, has a wide range of app integration, and includes a web version called "MELON by Streamlabs" that enables streaming from any device. It is the greatest way to connect through apps like Facebook, Twitch, and YouTube.

We will use Streamlabs Obs because it is very easy to use and navigate. It is a live streaming platform that can show your streams to others using the platform. Steamlabs Obs has a large variety of features while still being beginner-friendly. We figured that the apps it connects with would be best suited for our work.
